About This Series – Ages 3–5

The Tiny Tools for Big Feelings series for ages 3 to 5 is designed to help young children build essential emotional and behavioral skills during their early developmental years. Through engaging stories and practical exercises, each booklet focuses on a specific skill, such as calming down, listening, following directions, speaking honestly, cooperating with others, paying attention, and organizing personal belongings. These foundational tools encourage self-regulation, positive communication, and social interaction in a way that is playful, age-appropriate, and easy to understand. This series supports both children and caregivers in navigating early emotional experiences with confidence and care.
